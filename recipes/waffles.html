How to make Waffles

Making yummy waffles. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Waffle ingredients

The AllRecipes community adores this waffle recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1,5 cups warm milk
  • third cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla.
  3. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  4. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron.
  5. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
